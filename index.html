Navigating Digital Assets​
Institutional-Grade Advisory for UHNWIs & Family Offices

In an era where digital assets are reshaping global finance, sophisticated investors require more than just access; they demand security, compliance, and expert guidance. CONSENSUS CAPITAL, a FINMA-regulated advisory firm based in Geneva, offers unparalleled expertise to ultra-high-net-worth individuals, family offices, funds, and corporations seeking to integrate digital assets into their portfolios strategically.​

Led by globally respected industry veterans with deep roots across blockchain, digital asset markets, and finance, we bridge the gap between traditional wealth management and the burgeoning digital economy.
